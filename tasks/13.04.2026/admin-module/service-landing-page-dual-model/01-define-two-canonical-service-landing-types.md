# 01 Define Two Canonical Service Landing Types

## Goal

Define the two official landing page models and prevent ad hoc duplication.

## Required Models

### Service plus specific diviner

This page answers:

- what the service is
- why this diviner is the right fit
- how to book this exact diviner for this service

No diviner-selection step is needed.

### Service only

This page answers:

- what the service is
- which diviners offer it
- how the customer chooses among them

This page must hand off into diviner selection.

## Rule

These should be treated as two first-class public page models, not one overloaded page with accidental branching.

## Deliverables

- page-model definitions
- ownership boundaries
- conversion intent differences between the two models
