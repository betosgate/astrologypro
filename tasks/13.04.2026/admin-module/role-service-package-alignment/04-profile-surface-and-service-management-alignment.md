# 04 Profile Surface and Service Management Alignment

## Goal

Ensure profile and service-management surfaces reflect the same package-based category permissions.

## Required Behavior

Diviner and trainee profile-related service surfaces should only show the categories enabled by the resolved package.

This applies to:

- profile edit or overview surfaces
- service setup or service list surfaces
- public-facing previews where relevant

- service-management constraints
- public preview alignment rules
