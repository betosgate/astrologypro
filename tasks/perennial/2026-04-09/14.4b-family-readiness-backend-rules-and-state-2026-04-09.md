## Goal

Define backend readiness rules for family chart and transit lifecycle.

## Natal Ready (per member)

1. `date_of_birth`
2. `birth_time`
3. `birthLocationLabel`
4. `birthLat`
5. `birthLng`
6. `birthTzone`

## Family Ready

1. Primary member is natal-ready.
2. Each included family member is natal-ready before member-specific outputs are generated.
3. `relationType` and `subRelation` must be valid for family members.

## Monthly Transit Ready

1. Natal chart exists for that member.
2. Membership status active.

## Acceptance

1. Family output is blocked for members with incomplete birth data.
2. Transit flow follows natal-generated requirement.
