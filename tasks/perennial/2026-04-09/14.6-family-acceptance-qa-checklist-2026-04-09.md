## Test Matrix

### A. Family Signup

1. Choose `Family` plan.
2. Verify only minimal fields shown.
3. Verify family relation fields shown and validated.
4. Verify birth location fields are captured during signup:
   - `birthLocationLabel`
   - `birthLat`
   - `birthLng`
   - `birthTzone`
5. Complete payment successfully.

### B. First Login Completion

1. Login after payment.
2. Verify completion form opens.
3. Verify signup fields prefilled for primary and family members.

### C. Birth Location Search

1. Search city for each member.
2. Select result and verify label/lat/lng/tzone stored.

### D. Readiness Gates

1. Verify member-specific outputs blocked until member birth data complete.
2. Verify natal/transit readiness states update after save.

### E. Security And Persistence

1. Verify save updates authenticated user context only.
2. Verify partial save and reload persistence.

## Exit Criteria

1. Family onboarding flow passes A-E.
2. No cross-user data update possible.
3. No login redirect loop.
