## Test Matrix

### A. Family Signup

1. Open website and click `GET START`.
2. Verify the first Perennial signup screen shows `Single`, `Couple`, and `Family`.
3. Choose `Family` plan.
4. Verify the page scrolls to or reveals the Family signup form below.
5. Verify only minimal basic fields shown.
6. Verify family relation fields shown and validated.
7. Verify birth fields are not captured during signup.
8. Complete payment successfully.

### B. First Login Completion

1. Login after payment.
2. Verify completion form opens.
3. Verify signup fields prefilled for primary and family members.
4. Verify `date_of_birth`, `birth_time`, `birthLocationLabel`, `birthLat`, `birthLng`, and `birthTzone` are present in the post-login form.

### C. Birth Location Search

1. Search city for each member.
2. Select result and verify label/lat/lng/tzone stored.

### D. Readiness Gates

1. Verify member-specific outputs blocked until member birth data complete.
2. Verify natal/transit readiness states update after save.

### E. Security And Persistence

1. Verify save updates authenticated user context only.
2. Verify partial save and reload persistence.
3. Verify post-login routing behavior is unchanged after completion.

## Exit Criteria

1. Family onboarding flow passes A-E.
2. No cross-user data update possible.
3. No login redirect loop.
