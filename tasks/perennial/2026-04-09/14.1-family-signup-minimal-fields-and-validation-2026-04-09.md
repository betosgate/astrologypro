## Goal

Reduce `Family` signup to essential basic fields for the main user and each added family member. Birth identity fields move to post-login completion.

## Primary Required Fields

1. `firstName`
2. `lastName`
3. `email`
4. `phone`
5. `gender`
6. `occupation`
7. `address`
8. `city`
9. `state`
10. `zip`
## Additional Family Member Required Fields

1. `relationType` (must be `Family`)
2. `subRelation` (valid family relation from UI)
3. `firstName`
4. `lastName`
5. `email`
6. `phone`
7. `gender`
8. `occupation`
9. `address`
10. `city`
11. `state`
12. `zip`
## Hide In Family Signup

1. `date_of_birth`
2. `birth_time`
3. `birthLocationLabel`
4. `birthLat`
5. `birthLng`
6. `birthTzone`
7. Optional questionnaire block
8. Post-login guidance questions

## Validation

1. Required fields for primary member and each added family member.
2. Email format validation.
3. Phone format validation.
4. Zip 5-digit validation.
## Acceptance

1. Family signup completes with minimal required fields only.
2. Birth identity fields are deferred to post-login completion.
3. Deep guidance fields are deferred to post-login completion.
