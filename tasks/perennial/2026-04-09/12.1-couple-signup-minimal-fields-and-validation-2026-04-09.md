## Goal

Reduce `Couple` signup to essential basic fields only for both members.

## Primary Required Fields

1. `firstName`
2. `lastName`
3. `email`
4. `phone`
5. `gender`
6. `occupation`
7. `address`
8. `city`
9. `state`
10. `zip`

## Partner Required Fields

1. `relationType` (must be `Couple`)
2. `subRelation` (`Husband` or `Wife`)
3. `firstName`
4. `lastName`
5. `email`
6. `phone`
7. `gender`
8. `occupation`
9. `address`
10. `city`
11. `state`
12. `zip`

## Hide In Couple Signup

1. `dateOfBirth`
2. `birthTime`
3. Optional questionnaire block

## Validation

1. Required fields for both members.
2. Email format + uniqueness across both members.
3. Phone format validation.
4. Zip 5-digit validation.

## Acceptance

1. Couple signup completes with only minimal basic data.
2. Birth/guidance fields are deferred to post-login completion.
