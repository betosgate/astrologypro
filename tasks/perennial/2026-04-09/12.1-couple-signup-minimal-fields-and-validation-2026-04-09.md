## Goal

Reduce `Couple` signup to essential basic fields for both members. Birth identity fields move to post-login completion.

## Primary Required Fields

1. `firstName`
2. `lastName`
3. `email`
4. `phone`
5. `gender`
6. `occupation`
7. `address`
8. `city`
9. `state`
10. `zip`
## Partner Required Fields

1. `relationType` (must be `Couple`)
2. `subRelation` (`Husband` or `Wife`)
3. `firstName`
4. `lastName`
5. `email`
6. `phone`
7. `gender`
8. `occupation`
9. `address`
10. `city`
11. `state`
12. `zip`
## Hide In Couple Signup

1. `date_of_birth`
2. `birth_time`
3. `birth_location_label`
4. Optional questionnaire block

## Validation

1. Required fields for both members.
2. Email format + uniqueness across both members.
3. Phone format validation.
4. Zip 5-digit validation.

## Acceptance

1. Couple signup completes with basic information only.
2. Birth identity fields and deep guidance fields are deferred to post-login completion.
