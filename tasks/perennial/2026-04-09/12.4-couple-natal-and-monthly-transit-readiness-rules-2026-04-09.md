## Goal

Define readiness gates for couple chart/transit lifecycle.

## Natal Ready (per member)

Required:

1. `date_of_birth`
2. `birth_time`
3. `birth_location_label`
4. `birth_lat`
5. `birth_lng`
6. `birth_tzone`

## Couple Compatibility Ready

1. Both members natal-ready.
2. `relationType` and `subRelation` valid.

## Monthly Transit Ready

1. Natal chart exists for member.
2. Membership status active.

## UI Gating

1. Block couple compatibility until both natal-ready.
2. Show per-member completion status.
3. Show CTA to complete missing fields.

## Acceptance

1. Compatibility output blocked until both members complete required birth data.
2. Transit flow follows natal-generated requirement.
