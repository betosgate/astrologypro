## Goal

Define validation and payload shape for signup birth location.

## Required Payload Keys

1. `birth_location_label`
2. `birth_lat`
3. `birth_lng`
4. `birth_tzone`

## Validation Rules

1. `birth_location_label` cannot be empty.
2. `birth_lat` range: `-90..90`.
3. `birth_lng` range: `-180..180`.
4. `birth_tzone` cannot be empty.
5. Free-text label without mapped lat/lng/tzone is invalid.

## Acceptance

1. Invalid location payload blocks submit.
2. Valid location payload proceeds to checkout.
