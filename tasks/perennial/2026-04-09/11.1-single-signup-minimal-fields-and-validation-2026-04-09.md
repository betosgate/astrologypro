## Goal

Reduce `Single` plan signup form to basic fields only.

## Scope

Applies only when selected plan is `single`.

## Show In Signup (Single)

1. `firstName`
2. `lastName`
3. `email`
4. `phone`
5. `gender`
6. `occupation`
7. `address`
8. `city`
9. `state`
10. `zip`

## Hide In Signup (Single)

1. `dateOfBirth`
2. `birthTime`
3. Optional questionnaire block

## Validation Rules

1. All 10 fields above required.
2. Email format required.
3. Phone format required.
4. Zip exactly 5 digits.
5. Keep first-invalid-field scroll/focus behavior.

## UX Copy

1. Explain profile can be completed after first login.
2. Keep generated-password-by-email note.

## Acceptance

1. On Single plan, birth fields and questionnaire are not shown.
2. Signup succeeds with only basic fields.
3. Validation errors are clear and focused.
